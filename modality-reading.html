What are Modalities?

The three modalities (also called qualities) describe HOW you approach life and handle change:

  • Cardinal Signs (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn) - The Initiators: Start things, lead, create change
  • Fixed Signs (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius) - The Sustainers: Maintain, stabilize, resist change
  • Mutable Signs (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces) - The Adapters: Adjust, transition, embrace change

Your modality balance reveals your natural approach to life's cycles and challenges.
