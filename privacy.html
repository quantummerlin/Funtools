🔒 Privacy Policy

How we protect your cosmic data and personal information

Our Privacy Promise

🛡️ Privacy First Quantum Merlin is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your spiritual journey is personal, and your data should stay that way.

1. Information We Collect

Good news: We collect minimal information. Here's what we use:

  • Birth dates and names - Only used locally in your browser to generate readings. We do NOT store this on any server.
  • Profile data - Stored only in your browser's local storage. Never sent to us.
  • Tool usage - Tracked locally in your browser to show your journey progress. We don't see this.

2. Local Storage

Quantum Merlin uses your browser's localStorage to:

  • Save your profiles for convenience
  • Remember which tools you've used
  • Store your preferences

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it anytime through your browser settings.

3. What We DON'T Do

  • ❌ We don't sell your data
  • ❌ We don't track you across websites
  • ❌ We don't store your birth information on our servers
  • ❌ We don't share your information with third parties
  • ❌ We don't require account creation

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for essential functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use:

  • Google Fonts - To display our beautiful typography
  • Hosting services - To serve the website (standard web hosting)

These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your local data at any time (browser settings → clear site data)
  • Use our tools without providing real personal information
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

7. Children's Privacy

Quantum Merlin is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the effective date.
