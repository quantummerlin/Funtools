üìñ Understanding Your Signs
Your Sun Sign (most people know this) represents your core identity and ego. It's based only on your birth date.
Your Moon Sign reveals your emotional nature, inner world, and deepest needs. It changes signs every 2-3 days, so you need your birth date and ideally your birth time for accuracy.
Your Rising Sign (Ascendant) shows how others perceive you and your approach to life. It changes every 2 hours, so exact birth time is essential for accuracy.

What You'll Need

  • Birth Date: Month, day, and year
  • Birth Time: As exact as possible (check birth certificate)
  • Birth Location: City and country
