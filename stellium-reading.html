What is a Stellium?

A stellium occurs when 3 or more planets gather in the same zodiac sign or house, creating an intense concentration of energy. This cosmic cluster becomes a dominant theme in your life, amplifying the qualities of that sign and creating a powerful focal point for your soul's journey.
